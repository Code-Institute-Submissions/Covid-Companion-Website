About Us

Covid Companion was set up to provide help for both old people and people who are classed as vulnerable during the current Covid-19 (Coronavirus) pandemic. Our goal is to connect the old and vulnerable with people who are willing to volunteer their time to retrieve essential goods such as food and medication for those in need during these unprecedented times.

Volunteering

If you're available and able to volunteer any amount of time whether its helping 10 people or 1 person a week, all help is greatly appreciated. In times like these we do not know what is coming next, so coming together to ensure everyone is being taken care of is the most important thing. If you're interested in volunteering we urge you to create an account and get started with us!

Needing Assisstance

We understand for many people asking for any type of help is not an easy task however during times like these we are all here to support eachother, especially to those in need. If you're in need of assisstnace, we urge you to register an account and create a post so that one of our volunteers can help. You can provide a list of items that you need in the shops or you provide us with your click and collect order number with peace of mind as all our volunteers have been verified.

How to get Started

Whether you're looking to become a volunteer or in need of someone to do your weekly shop as you are following the HSE's "cocooning" reccomendations, the process is the same and the process is simple. All you need to do to get involved is create an account and you're all set to go!
